Roots in Romania

Born in Iași, I spent most of my childhood glued to video games, forever poking at their edges to discover shortcuts, cheats, and glitches. That restless urge to see what makes a system tick—and how it can be bent—eventually steered me toward technology and, later on, AI.

Education in Italy

Moved to Milan where I completed my IT high school diploma while earning certifications in network security and cloud computing. This period honed my technical skills and ignited my AI passion.

Innovation in Netherlands

At NHL Stenden University of Technology, I researched and implemented neural network optimization techniques. Collaborated on EU-funded AI projects focusing on natural language processing and more less-AI projects.

Breakthrough in USA

Currently working remotely in the states, my work focuses on making advanced AI accessible while ensuring ethical and safe implementation.

Philosophy & Approach

My multicultural journey taught me that truly transformative AI must balance technical excellence with human-centered design. I believe the most powerful solutions emerge when diverse global perspectives converge around challenging problems.

Today, I bring this holistic approach to every project, ensuring our AI solutions are not just technically sound but culturally aware and ethically grounded.

Pushing AI Boundaries

I partner with the teams behind today's flagship AI assistants—including OpenAI's ChatGPT, xAI's Grok, Anthropic's Claude, Astral, Google DeepMind's Gemini—to push every new release closer to human-level performance.

Remote Team Orchestration

Operating 100% remotely, I coordinate large, globally distributed annotation and research squads through a blend of asynchronous workflows and live "war-room" Google-Meet sessions.

Core Responsibilities:

  • Designing & iterating evaluation frameworks that expose blind spots in reasoning, safety, and factuality
  • Translating bleeding-edge research into production guidelines
  • Coaching reviewers and auditing output to refine training data
  • Partnering with engineering leads to triage urgent issues

Career Evolution

I've progressed from Attempter to Reviewer—now on the front line of quality control for models used by millions daily.

Gatekeeper Role:

As a Reviewer, I help safeguard releases of ChatGPT, Grok, Claude, Astral, and Gemini.

Elevated Responsibilities:

  • Designing stress-tests for model resilience
  • Leading critical "war-room" diagnostics
  • Approving fixes before deployment

The promotion recognizes past contributions—like boosting robustness scores double-digits release over release—and empowers me to mentor new team members.

It's a bigger stage, tighter deadlines, and deeper influence—driving me to ensure these flagship models remain accurate, safe, and genuinely helpful.
